Cinnaroll

Description

I watched mom make these rolls when I was young and have always loved how easy they are to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 (.25 oz) packages active dry yeast

  • 6 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup veg. oil
